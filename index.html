Welcome to Helping Hands

"Helping Hands are better than praying lips"

Helping hands is a group of educated youth eager to serve the people. Our mission is to help the students in their career path on various levels, Welfare of society is our motto and we believe in action rather than only preach.

We help students in following ways:

  • Awareness program.
  • Motivational program.
  • Counselling and Guidance program
  • Mentorship Program
  • Competition Tests
  • Felicitation ceremony
  • Free health camp.
  • Conducting classes for various entrance exams

Education is the most powerful weapon you can use to change the world and the most effective way to break out of the cycle of poverty. Yet,.India has millions of youth of secondary and higher secondary school-going age dropping out of school. Whenever there is a major imbalance, NGOs exist to balance the see-saw in times of crises. NGOs like HELPING HANDS play a significant role in ensuring that every child, especially those from vulnerable communities, receive the opportunity to build a better future through education. . HELPING HANDS is an NGO that works in states of Uttar Pradesh to guide the vulnerable and underprivileged students in choosing their right career path and empower them to break the cycle of poverty.
